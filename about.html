Introduction to the kwụrụ ọtọ series

kwụrụ ọtọ is the first official footwear series of Colin Mariko.

Colin Mariko is an African fashion brand that tells a story behind every design she creates.


"Kwụrụ ọtọ" is the Ibo translation to "stand up"

The story behind the Kwuru oto series was birthed as a result of the end SARS protest. It was the first time Nigerians,especially the youth, stood together to fight for a particular cause.

The story behind the Kwụrụ ọtọ series was birthed as a result of the end SARS protest. It was the first time Nigerians,especially the youth, stood together to fight for a particular cause.

Every component in the design is symbolic to this message:
- Stand together
- Complete the gap
- Barriers would be present
- Rise above it.

On our beautiful design KWURU OTO, there is a loafer sewing running across the tongue region and is complemented by tassles decorated with bead-like fancies.

The loafer sewing depicts people standing together. The tassles completes the gap, encouraging the efforts of those standing together.

They are in seven bunches, representing the seven letters "STAND UP"

Running from both sides of the Kwuru oto design is a belt that signifies barriers intending to disrupt the efforts of those standing together.

Two bunches of tassles however rises above the belt.

This denotes that for every good cause, there must be obstacles but as conquerors that we are, we rise above any situation.

Kwụrụ ọtọ is for every one, irrespective of the tribal, cultural and religious differences. Having a pair on, will not only beautify your feet but also serve as a reminder that we are all we have.

Kwụrụ ọtọ can be rocked in shorts.
